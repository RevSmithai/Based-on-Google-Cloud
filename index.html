Case Study

How Ewake AI helped a SaaS company reduce MTTR by 65% using Google Cloud integration.

The Challenge

A growing SaaS company was experiencing increasing production incidents with an average MTTR of 2.5 hours. Their engineering team spent 40% of their time debugging production issues instead of building new features.

The Solution

Ewake AI was implemented with Google Cloud Monitoring, Logging, and BigQuery integration. The platform used Vertex AI for anomaly detection and Pub/Sub for real-time event processing.

The Results

  • 65% reduction in MTTR (from 2.5h to 53min)
  • 40% decrease in false positive alerts
  • 30% more time for feature development
  • Seamless integration with existing Google Cloud infrastructure
